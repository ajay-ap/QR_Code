why choose QR?

QR Code is two-dimensional barcode which is categorized in matrix barcode that can store data information. QR stands for “Quick Response” as it intend the code to allow its contents to be decoded at high speed.

This kind of barcode was initially used for tracking inventory in vehicle parts manufacturing and is now used in a variety of industries. Quick Response (QR) codes seem to appear everywhere these days. We can see them on posters, magazine ads, websites, product packaging and so on.

Using the QR codes is one of the most intriguing ways of digitally connecting everyone to the internet via mobile phones since the mobile phones have become a basic necessit thing of everyone.
